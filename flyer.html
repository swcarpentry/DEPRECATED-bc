Do you spend too much time wrestling with computers,
and not enough doing research?
We can help.

Our mission is to teach basic lab skills for research computing so that scientists, engineers, librarians, and others can get more done in less time. Our volunteer instructors deliver over a hundred two-day events a year for thousands of of people, and cover:

  • The Unix Shell for automating repetitive tasks
  • Git and GitHub for tracking and sharing work
  • Python or R for building programs reliably
  • Databases, SQL, and data management

Our lessons are freely available under the Creative Commons - Attribution license, and we'd be happy to talk about how we could help you: please check out our web site, send us email, follow us on Twitter as @swcarpentry, or fork us on GitHub.
